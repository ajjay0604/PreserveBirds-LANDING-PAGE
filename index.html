About Us





OUR MISSION

At Preserve Birds, our mission is to protect and conserve the diverse bird species that inhabit our planet. We strive to create a world where birds can thrive in their natural habitats, free from the threats of human activity, climate change, and habitat destruction.










OUR VISION

We envision a future where the harmonious existence of birds and humans is not only possible but is a reality. By fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of birds, we aim to inspire collective action towards their conservation.







WHO WE ARE ?

Preserve Birds is a dedicated group of environmentalists, bird enthusiasts, and volunteers who share a common passion for avian life. Our team comprises experts in ornithology, conservation biology, and environmental science, as well as community members committed to making a difference.







OUR HISTORY

Founded in 2021, Preserve Birds began as a small initiative by a group of bird lovers who wanted to address the growing threats to bird populations. Over the years, we have grown into a reputable organization known for our effective conservation strategies and community-driven efforts.




Services

Wildlife Rescue & Rehabilitation

Our team provides medical treatment and rehabilitation for injured or sick birds, helping them return to the wild.

Education &
Outreach Programs

We offer educational programs and workshops to raise awareness about bird conservation and inspire action.
